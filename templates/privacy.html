Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using Spendly:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Financial Data: Expense entries including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions
  • Usage Information: Data about how you interact with our service for analytics and improvement

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers or credit card details.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to provide and improve our service:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your expense tracking data
  • To generate spending insights and reports
  • To communicate with you about service updates or issues
  • To protect the security and integrity of our platform

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

You may export or delete your data at any time through your account settings. Upon account deletion, we retain your data for 30 days before permanent removal, except where retention is required by law.

Third Party Services

Our service may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as analytics tools or payment processors) that are not operated by us. We recommend reviewing the privacy practices of any third-party services you access.

We use only trusted service providers who adhere to strict data protection standards and are contractually bound to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Contact Us

Email: privacy@spendly.example.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days and work with you to resolve any concerns.
